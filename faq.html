FAQ

Policies and answers

Can I return my cake?

No. Cakes cannot be returned after purchase and opening the package.

How do I order?

Order by phone or email only. We do not process payments online yet.

What payment methods do you accept?

Mpesa or cash on pickup.

What are the pickup hours?

Pickup is available during business hours; please collect within 12 hours.

Return policy

Because cakes are perishable and personal, we do not accept returns once the package has been opened. Please review your order details before pickup or delivery.
